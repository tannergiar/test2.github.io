My Story

Tanner Giar is DJ TANG. Tanner began in the entertainment industry in 2005 doing lighting for a Children's Theatre. From there, with his passion for event lighting and entertainment, Lumen 8 Productions was born. While providing lighting, sound, and design for events, Tanner found himself becoming more involved with the music of each event. He took this and turned it into a skill.

DJ TANG has been performing at events for the past 10 years. He has headlined parties of thousands and created lasting memories with countless wedding couples. DJ TANG specializes in spinning Top 40, Hip-Hop, and EDM. His sets can be recongized by the unique mix of the new and the old.

Bring your event to the next level with DJ TANG. Keep your dancefloor packed till the end of the night with your favorite in Top 40, Hip-Hop- and EDM. DJ TANG will not disappoint.
