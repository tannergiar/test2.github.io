Bookings
DJ TANG Contact Social Production

Every event is unique and deserves the best. DJ TANG is dedicated to providing you with exactly what you want for your event.

The booking process is simple. Contact DJ TANG through one of the ways below. DJ TANG will then discuss the specifics of your event and provide a quote for the services you require. Once you approve the quote, you will recieve a contract to sign in order to reserve your date. DJ TANG will reach out to you as your event date approaches to ensure that everything is perfect.

Have a last minute event? No problem! Contact DJ TANG to check availability. However, it is highly reccomended that you book DJ TANG well in advance as dates usually fill up fast.

If you need more than a DJ, contact Lumen 8 Productions. Lumen 8 Productions is owned and operated by DJ TANG.
